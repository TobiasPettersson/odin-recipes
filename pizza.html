Authentic italian pizza

  • 2 dl flour
  • 1 italian
  • 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 250g Mozzarella
  1. Mix water and flour in a bowl to a dough.
  2. Knead the dough around the italian.
  3. Put some tomato sauce and mozzarella on it and put in the oven.
  4. When the italian stops screaming the pizza is done.
