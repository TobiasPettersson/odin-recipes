Pancakes

Recipe:

Very good fluffy amazing wonderful beautiful pancakes.

  • 2 1/2 dl flour
  • 1/2 tspn salt
  • 6 dl milk
  • 3 eggs
  • Butter
  • Jam, berries or some other shit
  1. Mix water and flour in something. Whisk in half of the milk and make a smooth smet. Whisk in the rest of the milk and the eggs.
  2. Fry thin pancakes in some butter in a pan.
  3. Serve with jam, berries or some other shit.
